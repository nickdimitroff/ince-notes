## Basics of sound

- A sound wave is a disturbance in a medium, e.g., vibration in a solid or air pressure changes in air.
- The disturbance travels with a speed based on the medium's properties and mass.
- The air itself does not ove much, it oscillates in its general viscinity.
- The wave/disturbance moves, causing compressions/rarefactions of the air molecules in their local regions.
